Welcome to Plant Glycosider

Plant Glycosider will add sugar compounds to primary metabolites of plant to simulate the glycosylation process. This work is inspired by PlantMat

API
Rule GET: https://glcosider.biotransformerdb.ca/api/generate/{smiles string}
Example GET: https://glcosider.biotransformerdb.ca/api/generate/OC1=CC=CC=C1O
